Kaira

The Void Strider

Kaira's past is shrouded in the same darkness she commands. Once a member of the enigmatic Shadow Syndicate, she broke away to forge her own path. Her mastery over void energy allows her to teleport short distances, create illusory clones, and deliver fatal strikes from the shadows.

Abilities

  • Shadow Step: Instantly teleport to a nearby location.
  • Void Clone: Create a decoy that explodes on contact.
  • Umbral Strike: A powerful single-target attack that deals bonus damage from behind.
