About Me

A lifelong pursuit of impactful solutions and sustainable growth.

I am an entrepreneur and innovator driven by a passion for solving complex global challenges. My journey began with a simple belief: technology should be a force for good, empowering communities and creating opportunities for all.

Over the past two decades, I've founded several ventures in renewable energy, artificial intelligence, and global health, each aimed at creating scalable and sustainable change. My work is guided by a philosophy of first-principles thinking and a commitment to long-term impact over short-term gains.

Beyond business, my foundation focuses on educational reform and environmental conservation, striving to build a more equitable and resilient future for the next generation.
